Game Modes

Details about each game mode.


Spire

Build up to a floating green weapon and press C to grab it.

win

Win a round by eliminating the other team. First to win 5 rounds wins the match.

Static

All players start with a randomized loadout. Eliminate the other team with what you've got.

win

Win a round by eliminating the other team. First to win 5 rounds wins the match.

Quick Facts

Little bits of information to help you win the game.


Digging Blocks

Will give you more blocks to build with.

Get More Ammo

By picking up the blue floating object after a player dies.

Capture Canisters Faster

By having multiple teammates in the capture zone.
